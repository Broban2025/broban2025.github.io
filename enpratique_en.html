Practical Information

Transportation

If you have a car

The church and castle are located about 1 hour north of Paris, via the A1 motorway, less than 10 minutes apart.

If you can offer carpooling spots in your car, please don't hesitate to indicate it here

If you don't have a car

You can fill out this carpooling form so we can connect you with people who can offer rides

In any case

To avoid having to drive after the party (there might be some alcohol served), we will arrange shuttles for transportation:

  • From a hotel (that is currently being identified) to the church - Departure at 3:30 PM from the hotel parking lot
  • From the church to the castle - departure at 5:30 PM after the ceremony
  • From the castle to the hotel, first departure at 1 AM then at a frequency to be determined

This way you can first go to the hotel to check in, and the shuttles will take care of the rest!

Accommodation

We are currently looking for a hotel where we can gather after the celebration. We will try to secure preferential rates for the wedding guests.
