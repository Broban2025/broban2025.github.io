Practical Information

Transportation

If you have a car

The church and castle are located about 1 hour north of Paris, via the A1 motorway, less than 10 minutes apart.

If you can offer carpooling spots in your car, please don't hesitate to indicate it here

If you don't have a car

You can fill out this carpooling form so we can connect you with people who can offer rides

In any case

To avoid having to drive after the party (there might be some alcohol served), we will arrange a shuttle service which will include (the exact timetable will follow):

  • A bus transfer from the selected hotels (details below) to La Trinité Church in La Chapelle-en-Servale
  • Another bus transfer from the church to the Château de Pontarmé for the reception./li>
  • Overnight minivan rotations to bring you back to your hotel

This way you can first go to the hotel to check in, and the shuttles will take care of the rest!
