Practical Information

Transportation

If you have a car

The church and castle are located about 1 hour north of Paris, via the A1 motorway, less than 10 minutes apart.

If you can offer carpooling spots in your car, please don't hesitate to indicate it here

If you don't have a car

You can fill out this carpooling form so we can connect you with people who can offer rides

In any case, a shuttle service is planned

To avoid having to drive after the evening (there might be some alcohol served), shuttles will run as follows:

  • At 3:30 PM sharp, a bus will depart from the Best Western Escapade in Senlis to La Trinité Church in La Chapelle-en-Serval
  • At 3:30 PM sharp, a second bus will depart from in front of the Campanile hotel (which is a 5-minute walk from the Ibis Senlis and Ibis Budget)
  • At 5:45 PM sharp, both buses will go from the church to the Château de Pontarmé
  • Minivan rotations during the night can bring you back to your hotels, according to the following schedule:

Time Shuttle Departure
11:45 PM 1st shuttle departure
12:45 AM 2nd shuttle departure
1:45 AM 3rd shuttle departure
2:30 AM 4th shuttle departure
3:15 AM 5th shuttle departure
4:00 AM Last shuttle departure

You'll need to check in at your hotel first and the shuttles will take care of the rest!

Accommodation

Below is a selection of hotels divided into three categories:

Category 1: Hotels served by the shuttle – Budget-friendly options

  • Campanile Senlis 3 stars – From €71 per night (early deal rate).
  • Ibis Senlis 3 stars – From €96 per night.
  • Ibis Budget Senlis 2 stars – From €95 per night.

Category 2: Hotel served by the shuttle – Higher standard

  • Best Western Plus Escapade Senlis 4 stars – From €106 per night.

Category 3: Hotels not served by the shuttle – Various standards and price ranges

  • Relais d'Aumale 3 stars.
  • Intercontinental Chantilly at La Chapelle-en-Serval 5 stars. The most luxurious option near the wedding venue for those seeking a premium experience.
  • Mercure Chantilly 4 stars.
  • Hôtel du Parc Chantilly 3 stars.
  • Le Grand Pavillon Chantilly 4 stars.

    • There are of course many other diverse options (hotels, guesthouses, airbnbs) if you wish to conduct more extensive research.
